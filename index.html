About me

Vanessa Martin

My name is Vanessa and I am obsessed with all things type!
Born and raised in Mallorca (Spain), I studied business administration, market research and UI design, now I live and work in London.
I have always loved everything type. This love developed into my passion of using hand-drawn type and calligraphy. I don’t often go anywhere without a brush pen in my bag, always ready for inspiration. I am always thinking what is next for my prints and it is so darn exciting that they are starting to find their place in lovely homes! There’s nothing like having some motivational words in the home.
Check out my instagram @sparklettersvanessa to keep up with all of my latest work and behind the scenes videos!
